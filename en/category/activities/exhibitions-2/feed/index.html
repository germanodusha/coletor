Coletor » ExhibitionsColetor http://coletor.org/en Mon, 14 Nov 2016 21:11:55 +0000 en-US hourly 1 http://wordpress.org/?v=3.7.1 nascente noschese http://coletor.org/en/nascente-noschese/ http://coletor.org/en/nascente-noschese/#comments Mon, 25 Nov 2013 19:10:02 +0000 http://coletor.org/v3/?p=175 As part of the official schedule of Virada Cultural 2013, the narrow and forgotten Travessa Noschese  in central São Paulo was transformed, giving birth to a stream of activities able to discuss the lack of sociable places in the urban confusion and the unnecessary obsolescence that strikes certain cutouts of the state capital. The public street was taken by furniture made up with recycled materials and sheltered visual art and musical performances. Likewise, during the night we also occupied part of an ancient factory that today gives name to the street and functions as a mere parking lot.

Urban intervention: Partying Day.
Music: DJ’s Tahira, Piero Chiaretti, Ad Ferrera, Fernando Ticoulat, Leonardo Ruas, Marcio Vermelho e Jeronimo Sodré;  Mel Azul, Inky and EXZ.
Plastic interventions: Adler Murad; Conrado Zanotto; Guilherme Augusto (Gafi); Henrique Tomaz (RIEN); Julio Schaefer (Pas Schaefer); Pedro Sasso (Saci Loves You) and Vermelho.
Visual projections and installations: Pixiequark; Leo Stroka; Sala 28; Adriano Vanni; Lucas Schlosinski and Lilian Fontenla.
Collaboration: LAMB arts; Pixiequark and Partying Day.
Support: Virada Cultural 2013.

18/05/2013 – 19/05/2013
